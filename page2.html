The family / Team

Me and My Girl

We aim to defy all the negative expectations that are associated with the Building and Decorating trade by being timely, tidy, polite, respectful, sensibly priced and focused on a first class service in keeping with the highest standards of the Association of Master Tradesmen.

We have been established since 1989 and have undertaken many projects. We take care in every process from planning to executing, ensuring that the best results are always achieved.

We are a small connected family business with the capacity to run multiple projects throughout North, North West & West London, Middlesex, Herts & Buckinghamshire, with experience working in both small and large projects all to the highest of specifications.

We understand that our success comes from our relationships with our clients all of whom have direct access to us 6 days per week. A large percentage of our work is generated by referrals, showing that our customer base is a happy one.
